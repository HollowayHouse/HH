Welcome to the Holloway House. Originally, this place was home to the newly wealthy Holloway family in the early 1900s. However, after a string of tragedies plagued the family and their estate, the Holloways sold their estate to a couple who opened it up as a boarding house. In tune with the manor’s deadly reputation many residents of the home’s West Wing mysteriously passed and went missing. Eventually, the boarding house was closed down and abandoned.
Countless ghosts probably roam this estate, vengeful or weeping. If you listen carefully they say you can hear their cries.
As a member of your school's Occult Club, you will be venturing into the West Wing of Holloway House to explore its supernatural happenings. Hopefully, you survive along the way.
